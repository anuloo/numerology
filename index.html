What is numerology?

Numerology assigns to each letter of the alphabet a single-digit number ranging from 1 to 9, and each number has its own attributes and characteristics.

The vowels, consonants, and numbers in a name and birth date are totaled and reduced to a single digit to sum up a person’s nature. Numerology is said to provide insight and revelations, and there are various systems that have been used throughout the ages.

It was especially popular in Europe during the 18th century and continues to have a strong following today.

How does a name become a personal Numyana melody?

The Numyana app is your translator. It turns the letters you feed into it into numbers based on their numerology values, then transforms that sequence into a personal melody.

1. Name to numbers

Each letter in your name, birth date, nickname, or any word you enter is converted into a number using its numerology value, creating a unique string of digits.

2. Numbers to notes

Numyana maps those numbers to musical notes, so what you hear is a sequence of tones that mirrors the exact spelling you entered.

3. Notes to your melody

The app assembles these notes into a flowing composition. Each note relates back to a letter and its number, revealing the music encoded in your words.

What does the Numyana app do?

The Numyana app lets you hear your name as a melody and then tailor the sound, tempo, and rhythm using simple editing tools before you download it as a ringtone or share it with friends and family.

The app converts any text, including numerals and lucky numbers, into musical notes using numerology. Yours, your friends, loved ones, babies, pets, celebrities, and heroes — any name is game for Numyana.

Is my Numyana really unique?

We believe every Numyana melody is as personal as a fingerprint or a face. While it is technically possible for two people to share the same ringtone, the odds are very slim.

When you include first, middle, and last name — maybe a nickname — along with date of birth, the chance of replicating someone else’s Numyana profile becomes highly unlikely. You choose what to enter, and the app’s tools let you further shape sound, rhythm, and tempo to make your melody distinctly your own.
